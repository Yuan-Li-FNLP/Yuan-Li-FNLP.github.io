About Me 关于我

I am an M.S. student in Computer Science at Fudan University, advised by Prof. Xipeng Qiu. My research lies at the intersection of Large Language Models, Retrieval-Augmented Generation, and Reinforcement Learning.

我是复旦大学计算机学院的硕士研究生，导师是 邱锡鹏教授。 我的研究聚焦于大语言模型、检索增强生成与强化学习的交叉领域。

Before joining Fudan, I completed my undergraduate studies at Zhejiang University, Chu Kochen Honors College, majoring in Automation (Robotics) and Innovation Management as a second degree (GPA 3.95/4.00, ranked 2nd). During my time at ZJU, I was fortunate to work with Prof. Fei Gao on aerial robot swarms and Prof. You Wang on spherical robot control.

在加入复旦之前，我在浙江大学竺可桢学院完成了本科学习，主修自动化（机器人方向）， 并辅修创业管理第二学位（绩点 3.95/4.00，专业第二）。在浙大期间，我有幸跟随 高飞教授 研究空中机器人集群，并跟随 王酉教授 研究球形机器人控制。

I am also a National Level-2 Athlete in track and field, having won the bronze medal in men's high jump at the Shanghai Athletics Championships.

我也是一名国家二级运动员（田径项目），曾获上海市田径赛甲组男子跳高铜牌。
